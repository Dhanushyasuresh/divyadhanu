FAQ

Common questions

How do I customize this site?

Edit text and links directly in this HTML file. Change colors in the :root variables at the top of the CSS.

Can I add images?

Yes. Replace the placeholder Unsplash URLs with your image links or local files committed to the repo.

Will this work offline?

Yes—no external libraries required. Everything is in this file.

How do I add a new section?

Copy a section block, change the id, update the nav link, and style as needed.
